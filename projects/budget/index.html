Budget

Personal budgeting application built exclusively for Linux.

Current Version - Dev Alpha | Next Version - Public Alpha. Expected April, 2015

Introduction

Budget is your one stop shop for managing your finances. With Budget, record income and expenses into separate categories. Set a limit on each individual category so you never have to worry about over spending! Budget can also project your financial future. Plan your long term future with monthly and yearly projections of your financial health. Plan the current month with the daily and weekly projections.

Features

  • Record your income and expenses. Modify and delete them on the fly.
  • See an overview of all your spending.
  • Built using Python and Gtk+. Follows the Gnome Human Interface Guidelines (HIGS).
  • Ports on non-Linux systems are done so by enthusiastic community members, and are not maintained by mthx.
