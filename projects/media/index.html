Home Theatre

Intro

The purpose of this site is to provide comprehensive details on setting up a personal home theater solution.

This guide will be continuously updated as technology changes and advances. I'll be discussing everything from how to set up a dedicated media server all the way to the living room media center. We will talk about the importance of multi-copy/multi-location backups and some different options on how to do it. I'll also discuss different ways to digitize your physical media, and liberate your legally purchased DRM'ed media.
