Linux - NORLUG

I am a Linux enthusiast and advocator. I am the organizer of the Northfield Linux User Group (NORLUG) and the Northfield chapter of Coffee House Coders (CHC). After moving to the Northfield Minnesota area, I found that the local Linux User Group had more or less died out. So I decided to revive it, and started by hosting weekly coffee house coders events as part of NORLUG. Check out their website for the most current happenings with NORLUG.
