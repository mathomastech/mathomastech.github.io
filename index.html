Linux - NORLUG

I am a Linux enthusiast and advocator. I am the organizer of the Northfield Linux User Group (NORLUG) and the Northfield chapter of Coffee House Coders (CHC). After moving to the Northfield Minnesota area, I found that the local Linux User Group had more or less died out. So I decided to revive it, and started by hosting weekly CHC events as part of NORLUG. Check out their new website which I built (and which the redesign of this website was based off) for the most current happenings with NORLUG.

In addition to my general interest in everything Linux, Open Source, and Libre Software related, I am also a hardcore GNOME advocator. So much so in fact that I have every intention of becoming a GNOME contributor/developer as soon as my current set of projects are complete.
