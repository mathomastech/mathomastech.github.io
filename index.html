NORLUG - CHC

I am a Linux enthusiast and advocator. I am the organizer of the Northfield Linux User Group (NORLUG) and the Northfield chapter of Coffee House Coders (CHC). After moving to the Northfield Minnesota area, I found that the local Linux User Group has more or less died out. So I decided to revive it, and started by hosting weekly CHC events as part of NORLUG. There is a lot planned for NORUG in the 2014 year. Check out their new website which I built (and which the redesign of this website was based off) for the most current happenings with NORLUG.
